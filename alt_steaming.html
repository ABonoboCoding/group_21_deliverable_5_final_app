Steaming

Seperate the food from water

To steam foods, make sure the ingredients are not immersed in the water. Over a pot of water, add a collapsible pot or Chinese bamboo steamer, then add the foods you want to steam.

Boil the water

Cover, bring the water to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.

Time

Cooking times vary, depending on the foods being steamed.
